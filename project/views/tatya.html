Fact about Tatya Tope

✤Tantia Tope was a notable mutineer in the Indian Rebellion of 1857.
✤ Later on, Tantia Tope came to the relief of Rani Lakshmibai of Jhansi and with her seized the city of Gwalior.
✤Tatya Tope was executed by the British Government at Sipri on 18 April 1859.
✤Tantia Tatya Tope began to act in Nana Saheb's name from Bithur.
✤Tantia Tatya Tope was one of the leaders of the massacre of Cawnpore, which occurred on 27 June 1857.
✤Tatya Tope was able to induce the army of Tonk to join him.
✤Tatya Tope induced the state forces to rebel against the Raja and was able to replace the artillery he had lost at the Banas River.
