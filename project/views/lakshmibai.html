Fact about Lakshmibai

✤She was fondly called ‘Manu’ by everyone

The exact date of birth of Lakshmibai is still a topic of debate.

✤ Gangadhar was a very able ruler who significantly improved the financial condition of Jhansi

She got married to the king Jhansi,Gangadhar Rao Newalkar at the early age of 7 in may 1842 and was renamed Lakshmibai.

✤She was a braveheart from birth.

She was educated at home and her studies included shooting,horsemanship and fencing.
